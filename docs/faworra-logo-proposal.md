# Faworra Brand Identity: The Calm Operator (v2.0 Refinement)

**Design Rationale: Studio Faworra / Berlin**

Faworra is a Business Operating System for the West African SME. Following our review of the **v1.0 Brand Package (Tech Brutalist Minimal)**, we are evolving the identity to feel even more architectural, precise, and premium.

In Berlin, we define **Brutalism** not as "rough," but as **"honest."** We use clear grids, hard 90-degree angles, and a strict hierarchy to give the founder's mind "room to breathe."

---

## The Strict Brutalist Constraints
- **Radius:** 0px (Absolute hard edges).
- **Borders:** 1px solid #0A0A0A (Structural integrity).
- **Palette:** Obsidian (#0A0A0A), Ivory (#F8F7F2), Forest (#0D3B2E), Sandsong (#D4C5A8).
- **Typography:** Inter (Sans) for content, JetBrains Mono (Mono) for labels/metadata.

---

**Visual Metaphor:** A strictly geometric 'F' built on a 4x4 grid. It feels like an architectural blueprint or a structural beam. It represents the "Financial OS" and "Vault" as the unshakeable foundation of the business.
**Wordmark:** FAWORRA (Bold Caps, -0.04em tracking).

## Implementation Rationale

1.  **Concept 1 (The Grid)** is the "safest" and most authoritative. It fits perfectly into a dashboard sidebar.
2.  **Concept 2 (The Flow)** preserves the DNA of the original v1.0 branding but "Brutalist-ifies" the signal dot into a block.
3.  **Concept 3 (The Spine)** is the most unique. It speaks directly to the West African mission while maintaining a high-end Berlin technical aesthetic.
