Last updated July 23rd, 2024

### Introduction

The Mono Payment APIs is a suite of product that allows you to collect payments directly from customers’ bank accounts by leveraging the current bank transfer technology built into banking portals and mobile apps. This is a faster and more secure way for your customers to pay you without entering their card details. Before you can start integrating any of the Payment APIs, please note that you have to [request access to Payments](https://support.mono.co/en/articles/7066547-how-to-request-for-directpay) on your Mono dashboard.

The payment types available on Mono Payments include;

![DirectPay](/images/callout/bulb.png)

DirectPay

DirectPay is our one-time debit product that allows you to initiate a single charge to a customer's bank account. This is perfect for transactions where customers need to make a one-time payment without committing to a recurring arrangement. Immediately after a successful payment, a webhook is triggered to confirm the transaction, allowing you to seamlessly update your system.

![Direct Debit](/images/callout/bulb.png)

Direct Debit

Direct Debit is our recurring and subscription product that allows you set up mandate for recurring and future payments. With this product, you can set up recurring payments allowing customers to subscribe to your services with ease. You can also use this to manage loan repayments seamlessly and provide a hassle-free payment experience for your customers.
